What is it?

This split is a fairly simple training style that involves alternating between upper body muscles one day, and then lower body muscles the next. A typical week could look like this:

Monday
  1. Bench press
  2. Bent over row
  3. Incline Dumbell press
  4. Lat pulldown
  5. Dumbell shoulder press
  6. Preacher curl
  7. Cable pushdown
Tuesday
  1. Squat
  2. Camstring curl
  3. Leg press
  4. Calf raises
  5. Bulgarian split squats
Thursday
  1. Bent over row
  2. Flat dumbell press
  3. Plate-loaded lat pulll down
  4. Incline smith machine press
  5. EZ bar curl
  6. Barbell shoulder press
  7. Overhead tricep extension
Friday
  1. Legs
  2. Deadlift
  3. Legpress
  4. Hamstring curls
  5. Bulgarian split squats
  6. Calf raises
