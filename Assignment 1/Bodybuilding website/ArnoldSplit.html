  What is it?

Named after the legendary Arnold Schwarzenegger , this training style involves training opposite muscles to optimize the “pump” that is gotten.
A typical week would look like this:
Monday
  1. Dumbell bench press
  2. Lat pull down
  3. Low to high cable flies
  4. Seated cable row
  5. Chest dips (weighted or assisted if required)
  6. Dumbell rows
Tuesday
  1. Straight bar curl
  2. Barbell shoulder press
  3. Overhead tricep extension
  4. Preacher curl
  5. Lateral raises
  6. Cable pushdowns
  7. Alternating dumbell curls
  8. Rope tricep extensions
  9. Rear delt flies
Thursday
  1. Squat
  2. Deadlift
  3. Legpress
  4. Hamstring curls
  5. Bulgarian split squats
  6. Calf raises
Friday
  1. Dumbell benchpres
  2. Lat pull down
  3. Low to high cable flies
  4. Seated cable row
  5. Chest dips(weight or assisted if required)
  6. Dumbell rows
Saturday
  1. Straight bar curl
  2. Barbell shoulder press
  3. Overhead tricep extension
  4. Preacher curl
  5. Lateral raises
  6. Cable pushdowns
  7. Alternating dumbell curls
  8. Rope tricep extensions
  9. Rear delt flies
Sunday
  1. Squat
  2. Deadlift
  3. Legpress
  4. Hamstring curls
  5. Bulgarian split squats
  6. Calf raises
