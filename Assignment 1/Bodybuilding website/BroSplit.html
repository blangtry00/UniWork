What is it?

The Bro Split is called such because of its popularity from a younger male demographic who are training mainly to feel the “pump” as the main downside of this split is each muscle only gets hit once each week, nonetheless, this is how it would typically look.

Monday
  1. Bench Press
  2. Inlcine Dumbbell press
  3. Chest dips(Weighted or assisted)
  4. Peck deck
  5. Low to high cable flies
  6. Pushups
Tuesday
  1. Bent over Rows
  2. Lat pull down
  3. Machine row
  4. Cable row
  5. Shrugs
Thursday
  1. Straight bar curl
  2. Barbell shoulder pess
  3. Over head tricep extension
  4. Preacehr curl
  5. Lateral raises
  6. Cable pushdowns
  7. Alternating dumbell curls
