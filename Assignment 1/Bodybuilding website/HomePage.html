What is it?

Many types of training styles will get different results, body building splits typically have a focus on medium to high rep ranges. Even within the different goals, there are different ways to achieve them, here are a few different bodybuilding training splits.

Although some are > than others, they all have their place.

These include:
  • PPL(Push Pull Legs
  • Arnold Split
  • Upper Lower Split
  • Bro Split
Style Pros Cons
PPL(Push Pull Leg) Ability to hit muscle groups multiple times a week with good volume May result in more fatigue
Arnold Split Good "pump" due to muscles in the same areas being hit Current science doesn't really recommend this style due to it being hard to hit certain groups enough
Upper/Lower 4 sessions a week allows for every muscle to be hit twice addequetly Not much focus on upper body or the upper body days will be really long
Bro split Big focus on each muscle group per day Not enough weekly volume per group
